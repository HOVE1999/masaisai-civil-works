About Us

Masaisai Civil Works is a trusted construction and civil engineering company delivering reliable, high-quality services across Zimbabwe.

We specialize in construction projects, civil works, finishing, renovations and equipment hire, making us a dependable partner for residential, commercial and infrastructure development.

Our work is driven by quality, efficiency and client satisfaction. From site preparation and foundations to structural development and finishing, we provide end-to-end solutions tailored to each client’s needs.

We also offer well-maintained machinery for hire, including plate compactors, jumping jacks, jack hammers, concrete mixers and concrete pokers.

We pride ourselves on delivering projects on time, maintaining high standards of workmanship, offering transparent pricing and building long-term relationships with our clients.
