About Us

Masaisai Civil Works is a trusted construction and civil engineering company delivering reliable, high-quality services across Zimbabwe. We specialize in both construction projects and equipment hire, making us a dependable partner for residential, commercial, and infrastructure development.

Our work is driven by a commitment to quality, efficiency, and client satisfaction. From site preparation and foundations to structural development and finishing, we provide end-to-end solutions tailored to meet each client’s specific needs.

In addition to construction, we offer well-maintained machinery for hire, enabling individuals and contractors to execute projects efficiently and cost-effectively. Our equipment includes plate compactors, jumping jacks, jack hammers, concrete mixers, and concrete pokers.

We pride ourselves on delivering projects on time, maintaining high standards of workmanship, offering competitive pricing, and building long-term relationships with our clients.

Our mission is to provide durable construction solutions and accessible equipment hire services that empower our clients to build with confidence.
